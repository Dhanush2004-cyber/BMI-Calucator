What does this mean?

BMI is a simple index of weight-for-height used to classify underweight, overweight, and obesity in adults. It does not directly measure body fat and may not be accurate for athletes or those with high muscle mass.

  • Use BMI as a general guide, not a diagnostic tool.
  • Consider other indicators like waist circumference and body composition.
  • Consult a healthcare professional for personalized recommendations.
